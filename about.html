About Fuwalo

We're on a mission to revolutionize piano learning through technology, community, and innovation.

Our Mission

At Fuwalo, we believe that learning piano should be accessible, fun, and social. Our mission is to break down the barriers that prevent people from experiencing the joy of music.

Through innovative technology, we're creating a platform where anyone can learn piano at their own pace, connect with fellow musicians, and share their musical journey with the world.

Our Journey

How Fuwalo came to be

2023 Q1

The Idea

Alex, frustrated with traditional piano learning methods, envisioned a social, gamified approach to piano education that would make learning fun and accessible.

2023 Q2

First Prototype

The team built the first working prototype with basic piano functionality and simple multiplayer features. Initial user testing showed promising results.

2023 Q3

Beta Launch

Fuwalo beta was released to a select group of users. The response was overwhelming, with users praising the innovative approach to piano learning.

2023 Q4

Community Growth

Our Discord community grew to over 1,000 members. Users started creating and sharing their own MIDI files, building a rich library of content.

2024 Q1

Play Store Preparation

We're now preparing for our official Play Store launch, with enhanced features, improved performance, and a growing community of 2,500+ beta users.
