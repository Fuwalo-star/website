Getting Started

Getting started with Fuwalo is easy:

  1. Pre-register on Play Store or download the beta APK
  2. Create your account and complete the setup
  3. Take the skill assessment to get personalized lessons
  4. Start with the beginner lessons or explore the song library
  5. Join our Discord community for tips and support

Not at all! Fuwalo is designed for complete beginners as well as experienced players. Our adaptive learning system adjusts to your skill level, providing appropriate challenges whether you're just starting out or looking to improve your existing skills.

You only need your mobile device! Fuwalo works entirely on your phone or tablet. The virtual piano has realistic sound and touch response, making it perfect for learning and practice. No external piano or additional equipment required.

Features

The beta version includes:

  • Full virtual piano with realistic sound
  • MIDI file upload and playback
  • Basic multiplayer functionality
  • User accounts and progress tracking
  • Community sharing features
  • Performance recording

Fuwalo's multiplayer feature allows you to create or join rooms where multiple players can play the same song together in real-time. You can play with friends by sharing room codes or join public rooms to meet other musicians. The system synchronizes everyone's playing for a collaborative musical experience.

Yes! You can upload MIDI files to play your favorite songs. Simply import the MIDI file through the app, and Fuwalo will convert it into a playable format. You can then practice the song, record your performance, and even share it with the community.

Technical

For Android:

  • Android 8.0 or higher
  • 2GB RAM minimum (4GB recommended)
  • 500MB free storage space
  • Internet connection for multiplayer features

For iOS (coming soon):

  • iOS 13.0 or higher
  • iPhone 7 or newer
  • 500MB free storage space

If you're experiencing performance issues, try these solutions:

  • Close other apps to free up memory
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update to the latest version of Fuwalo
  • Clear the app's cache in your device settings

If problems persist, please contact our support team through Discord.

For the beta version, you'll need to download the latest APK from our website. When the app launches on Play Store, updates will be automatic through the Google Play Store. We'll notify you of new updates through our Discord community and in-app notifications.

Account

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Open the Fuwalo app
  2. Tap "Create Account" on the welcome screen
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile setup

Yes! Your progress, achievements, and saved songs are automatically synced to your account. Simply log in with the same account on any device, and your progress will be available. This works across Android devices and will work on iOS when it launches.

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login screen
  2. Tap "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

Community

Joining our Discord community is easy:

  1. Click the Discord link on our website or in the app
  2. Create a Discord account if you don't have one
  3. Accept the server rules and introduce yourself
  4. Start chatting with other piano learners!

Our Discord server has channels for different topics, including general chat, performance sharing, help and support, and community events.

You can share your performances in several ways:

  • Record your performance directly in the app
  • Upload the recording to the community section
  • Share it in our Discord server's performance channel
  • Post it on social media with #Fuwalo

The community loves to see progress and provide feedback, so don't be shy about sharing your performances!

Yes! We regularly host community events including:

  • Monthly competitions with prizes
  • Weekly community jam sessions
  • Beginner workshops and tutorials
  • Live Q&A sessions with the development team
  • Collaborative music creation challenges

Check our Discord server and the events section in the app for upcoming events and how to participate.
